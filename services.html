Products and Services

Eclipse Jute Packaging Nigeria Limited is in the worldwide jute industry to manufacture a wide range of jute products for clientsnot just in the Nigeria but also to all other parts of the world. We will do all that is permitted by the laws of the Fedral Republic of Nigeria to achieve our business goal, aim and ambition of starting the business. Our product offerings are listed below

Eclipse jute sacks

The Eclipse jute sack is an inexpensive bag, traditionally made from jute fibers. mostly used for packaging of procuts like cocoa, rice, food products & more

Eclipse Jute bags

The Eclipse Jute Bags are used in a more creative way, ideally suited to a variety of uses, ranging from multipurpose bags, beach bags

Eclipse jute fibres

Our Eclipse Jute Fibres are used mainly for clothing, cordage and sacking. it is factually one of the most resourceful and significant of all textile fibers.
