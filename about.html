Who We Are

Eclipse Jute Packaging Nigeria Limited is a joint owned jute production and marketing firm founded in the year 2020 with the head office located at 59A, Udeco Medical road, Chevyview Estate, Off Chevron drive, Lekki, Lagos. The main objective of the company is jute bag production, marketing and supply as a Whole.

Registered with The Nigerian Corporate Affairs Commision ( C.A.C ) of The Federal Republic of Nigeria with Registration Number RC:1719643, Eclipse jute packaging Nigeria Limited is Proud to work with customers large and small Worldwide.
